Reset your Affelios password in a few quick steps.

## How to reset your password

1. Visit <https://affelios.com/password-reset>
2. Enter the e-mail address associated with your Affelios account and click "Continue". This sends a password reset link to your inbox.
3. Open the "Affelios Password Reset" email and click "Set New Password".
4. Enter your new password. It must meet the following criteria:
   - At least 8 characters long
   - Contains at least one uppercase (A-Z) and one lowercase letter (a-z)
   - Contains at least one number (0-9)
   - Contains at least one special character (for example: !@#$%^&*)
5. Click "Continue" to confirm. You can now sign in with your new password.
