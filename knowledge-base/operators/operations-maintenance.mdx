# Operations and Maintenance

Ongoing operational procedures and maintenance tasks for managing a successful affiliate program.

## Daily Operations

### Monitoring and Alerts
1. **Performance Monitoring**
   - Review daily conversion metrics
   - Monitor traffic quality indicators
   - Check payment processing status
   - Verify system uptime and performance

2. **Alert Management**
   - Respond to automated alerts
   - Investigate anomalous activity
   - Address technical issues promptly
   - Escalate critical problems

3. **Affiliate Support**
   - Respond to affiliate inquiries
   - Process affiliate applications
   - Resolve tracking issues
   - Provide technical assistance

### Quality Assurance
1. **Traffic Quality Review**
   - Analyze conversion rates by affiliate
   - Identify suspicious traffic patterns
   - Review new affiliate performance
   - Monitor compliance with program terms

2. **Fraud Detection**
   - Investigate flagged conversions
   - Review click fraud alerts
   - Analyze affiliate behavior patterns
   - Take corrective actions as needed

## Weekly Operations

### Performance Analysis
1. **Program Metrics Review**
   - Analyze weekly performance trends
   - Compare metrics to targets and goals
   - Identify top and bottom performers

2. **Affiliate Performance Evaluation**
   - Review individual affiliate metrics
   - Identify optimization opportunities
   - Recognize high-performing partners
   - Address underperformance issues

### Relationship Management
1. **Affiliate Communication**

2. **Partnership Development**

   - Nurture existing relationships
