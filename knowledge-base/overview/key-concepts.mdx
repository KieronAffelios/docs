# Key Concepts

Understanding the fundamental concepts of affiliate marketing and the Affelios platform.

## Core Concepts

### Affiliate Program
An organized marketing effort where businesses reward partners (affiliates) for driving traffic or sales.

### Commission Structure
The framework that defines how affiliates are compensated for their promotional efforts.

### Tracking Links
Unique URLs that allow the platform to attribute traffic and conversions to specific affiliates.

### Conversion Tracking
The system that monitors and records when affiliate-driven actions result in desired outcomes.

## Platform-Specific Terms

### Operators
Businesses that create and manage affiliate programs on the Affelios platform.

### Affiliates
Marketing partners who promote operator programs and earn commissions.

### Traffic Sources
The various channels and methods affiliates use to drive traffic to operator offers.
