</Info>

## Subscription Cancellation Overview

<Card title="Cancellation Process" icon="calendar-xmark">
Canceling your Affelios subscription is straightforward and can be done entirely through your program dashboard. The cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, ensuring you maintain access until then.
</Card>

<AccordionGroup>

## Step-by-Step Cancellation Process

<Card title="Dashboard Cancellation" icon="gear">
Follow these steps to cancel your Affelios subscription through your program dashboard.
</Card>

### Cancellation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In to Affelios">
    Navigate to **https://affelios.com** and log in to your Affelios account using your credentials.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Programs Section">
    Click on **"Programs"** in the top menu or go directly to **https://affelios.com/programs**. This will display a list of all your Affelios programs.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Program to Cancel">
    Under the program you want to cancel, click on the **action icon (3 dots)** to open the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Billing Settings">
    Click on **"Billing"** in the dropdown menu. This will redirect you to the billing page for that specific program.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Locate Cancel Button">
    Scroll down to the bottom of the billing page where you'll find a large **red "Cancel Subscription" button**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Click the **"Cancel Subscription" button** and confirm your decision when prompted. The cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting Cancellation Issues

<Card title="Common Problems & Solutions" icon="triangle-exclamation">
Address frequently encountered issues during the subscription cancellation process.
</Card>

### Cancellation Problems

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Cannot Find Cancel Button">
    **Symptoms:**
    - Cancel button not visible on billing page
    - Billing page appears incomplete
    - Navigation issues accessing billing section
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Ensure you're logged into the correct account
    - Check if you have proper permissions for billing
    - Try refreshing the page or clearing browser cache
    - Contact support if button is missing
    - Verify you're on the correct program's billing page
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Cancellation Not Processing">
    **Symptoms:**
    - Clicked cancel but no confirmation received
    - Subscription still active after cancellation attempt
    - Error messages during cancellation process
    
    **Troubleshooting:**
    - Check internet connection and try again
    - Verify all required fields are completed
    - Clear browser cache and cookies
    - Try different browser or incognito mode
    - Contact support with specific error details
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Billing Access Issues">
    **Symptoms:**
    - Cannot access billing section
    - Permission denied errors
    - Missing billing information
    
    **Resolution:**
    - Verify account ownership and permissions
    - Check if account is in good standing
    - Ensure proper user role for billing access
    - Contact account administrator if applicable
    - Reach out to support for access restoration
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
