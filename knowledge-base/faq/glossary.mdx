# Glossary

Comprehensive definitions of affiliate marketing and Affelios platform terminology.

## General Affiliate Marketing Terms

### A
**Affiliate**
A marketing partner who promotes products or services for operators in exchange for commissions.

**Affiliate Agreement**
Legal contract outlining terms, conditions, and obligations between affiliates and operators.

**Affiliate ID**
Unique identifier assigned to each affiliate for tracking and attribution purposes.

**Affiliate Manager**
Person responsible for recruiting, managing, and supporting affiliate partners.

**Affiliate Network**
Platform that connects affiliates with multiple operators and programs.

**Attribution**
Process of crediting conversions to the appropriate affiliate or marketing touchpoint.

**Attribution Window**
Time period during which conversions can be attributed to an affiliate click.

### B
**Banner**
Graphical advertisement used by affiliates to promote operator offers.

**Bounce Rate**
Percentage of visitors who leave a website after viewing only one page.

**Brand Bidding**
Practice of bidding on branded keywords in paid search campaigns.

### C
**Click**
Action of a user clicking on an affiliate tracking link.

**Click Fraud**
Illegitimate clicking on affiliate links to generate false commissions.

**Click-Through Rate (CTR)**
Percentage of people who click on a specific link compared to total viewers.

**Commission**
Payment made to affiliates for successful referrals or conversions.

**Conversion**
Desired action taken by a referred customer (purchase, signup, etc.).

**Conversion Rate**
Percentage of clicks that result in successful conversions.

**Cookie**
Small text file stored on user's device to track affiliate attribution.

**Cookie Duration**
Length of time a tracking cookie remains active for attribution.

**Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)**
Amount paid for each successful conversion or customer acquisition.

**Cost Per Click (CPC)**
Amount paid for each click on an affiliate link.

**Creative**
Marketing material (banner, text link, video) used for promotion.

### D
**Dashboard**
Control panel interface showing performance metrics and management tools.

**Deep Link**
Direct link to specific product pages or sections within a website.

**Disclosure**
Required statement indicating affiliate relationship and compensation.

### E
**Earnings Per Click (EPC)**
Average commission earned per click across all conversions.

**Exclusive Offer**
Special promotion available only to selected affiliates.

### F
**First-Click Attribution**
Attribution model crediting the first affiliate interaction.

**Fraud Score**
Risk assessment rating for potentially fraudulent activity.

### G
**Geo-targeting**
Targeting specific geographic locations for campaigns.

### I
**Impression**
Single instance of an advertisement being displayed to a user.

**In-house Program**
Affiliate program managed directly by the operator company.

### K
**Key Performance Indicator (KPI)**
Metric used to evaluate affiliate program success.

### L
**Last-Click Attribution**
Attribution model crediting the final affiliate interaction.

**Landing Page**
Destination page where users arrive after clicking affiliate links.

**Lead**
Potential customer who has shown interest but hasn't purchased yet.

**Lifetime Value (LTV)**
Total revenue expected from a customer over their entire relationship.

**Link Cloaking**
Hiding or disguising affiliate links for better user experience.

### M
**Multi-Touch Attribution**
Attribution model distributing credit across multiple touchpoints.

### N
**Network**
Platform connecting multiple affiliates with various operators.

### O
**Operator**
Business or company that creates and manages affiliate programs.

**Organic Traffic**
Visitors arriving through unpaid search engine results.

### P
**Pay-Per-Click (PPC)**
Commission model paying for each click generated.

**Pay-Per-Lead (PPL)**
Commission model paying for each qualified lead generated.

**Pay-Per-Sale (PPS)**
Commission model paying percentage or fixed amount per sale.

**Payout**
Payment made to affiliates for earned commissions.

**Performance Marketing**
Marketing approach focused on measurable results and ROI.

**Pixel**
Tracking code used to monitor conversions and user behavior.

**Postback**
Server-to-server communication for conversion tracking.

### Q
**Quality Score**
Rating assessing traffic quality and affiliate performance.

### R
**Referral**
Customer directed to operator through affiliate marketing efforts.

**Retargeting**
Marketing to users who previously visited but didn't convert.

**Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**
Revenue generated per dollar spent on advertising.

**Return on Investment (ROI)**
Profit generated relative to investment made.

### S
**Sub-affiliate**
Affiliate recruited by another affiliate (in multi-tier programs).

**Sub-ID**
Additional tracking parameter for campaign segmentation.

### T
**Tracker**
A tool used by affiliates to segregate attributions by traffic source or campaign, allowing them to analyze which promotional efforts perform better than others.

**Tracking Link**
Special URL containing parameters to track affiliate attribution, generated when an affiliate creates a tracker and selects a brand/product combination.

**Traffic**
Visitors directed to operator websites through affiliate efforts.

**Traffic Source**
Origin or method through which visitors arrive (search, social, etc.).

### U
**Unique Click**
First click from a specific user within defined time period.

**URL**
Web address of a page or tracking link.

### V
**Voucher Code**
Discount code provided to customers through affiliate promotions.

### W
**Webhook**
Automated HTTP callback for real-time data synchronization.

**White Label**
Customizable platform branded with operator's identity.

## Affelios-Specific Terms

### A
**Affelios Dashboard**
Main interface for managing affiliate activities on the platform.

**Affelios ID**
Unique identifier for users within the Affelios system.

### C
**Campaign Manager**
Tool for creating and managing promotional campaigns.

**Creative Library**
Repository of marketing materials available to affiliates.

### P
**Program Marketplace**
Directory of available affiliate programs on Affelios.

### R
**Real-time Reporting**
Live performance data and analytics updates.

### T
**Tier System**
Performance-based classification levels for affiliates.

**Traffic Source Tracking**
Detailed monitoring of visitor origins and behavior.

## Technical Terms

### A
**API (Application Programming Interface)**
Set of protocols for building and integrating software applications.

**Attribution Model**
Framework for assigning conversion credit to marketing touchpoints.

### C
**CDN (Content Delivery Network)**
Distributed server network for faster content delivery.

**Cross-Device Tracking**
Monitoring user behavior across multiple devices.

### E
**Encryption**
Security process of converting data into coded format.

### J
**JavaScript**
Programming language for interactive web functionality.

**JSON**
Data interchange format for API communications.

### S
**SDK (Software Development Kit)**
Tools for building applications with specific platforms.

**SSL (Secure Sockets Layer)**
Security protocol for encrypted internet connections.

### U
**UTM Parameters**
Tracking codes added to URLs for campaign analysis.

## Compliance Terms

### F
**FTC (Federal Trade Commission)**
US agency regulating advertising and disclosure requirements.

### G
**GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)**
European privacy law governing personal data processing.

### P
**PCI DSS**
Security standard for handling credit card information.

### T
**Terms of Service**
Legal agreement outlining platform usage rules and conditions.

## Performance Metrics

### A
**Average Order Value (AOV)**
Mean purchase amount per transaction.

### C
**Churn Rate**
Percentage of affiliates who stop participating over time.

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
Total cost to acquire a new customer.

### L
**Lead Quality Score**
Rating assessing the value and likelihood of lead conversion.

### R
**Revenue Per Visitor (RPV)**
Average revenue generated per website visitor.

## Legal and Compliance

### C
**CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)**
California law governing consumer privacy rights.

**Compliance**
Adherence to legal requirements and platform policies.

### D
**Data Protection**
Measures to secure and properly handle personal information.

**Disclosure Requirements**
Legal obligations to reveal affiliate relationships.

### P
**Privacy Policy**
Document outlining data collection and usage practices.

This glossary provides essential terminology for understanding affiliate marketing and the Affelios platform. Terms are regularly updated to reflect industry changes and platform developments.
